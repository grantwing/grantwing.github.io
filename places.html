Some places I like from living in Philly, New York, Boston, and beyond.

Philadelphia, PA



🍩🥧🍪 Bieler's Baker - superb donuts and other sweet treats sold in Reading Terminal market
☕🥯🍳 Cafe Lift - one of my go to brunch spots in Philly
🇲🇽🌮🍸 El Purepecha - authentic Mexican food; try the al pastor tacos/burritos
🇮🇹🍝 L'anima - great Italian food in South Philly with a nice outdoor seating arrangement; BYOB
🍻 Love City Brewing - one of my favorite Philly breweries
🇮🇹🍝 Mangia Macaroni - small, casual Italian eatery with authentic pasta dishes
🇯🇵🍣🍻 QU JAPAN BISTRO & BAR - high quality, affordable sushi and other Japanese cuisine with a great HH

New York, NY


East Village

🍕 Baker's Pizza - above average NYC pizza
🍺🥃🥪 Ferns - neighborhood bar, good bites, great foliage
🍻 Good Beer - casual beer shop with great rotation of beers on tap
🍳🥓☕ POST - cozy brunch spot with homemade biscuits
🍜🥃 Tabetomo - good ramen
🥘🍲🥤 Thursday Kitchen - Korean style tapas, dirty Capri Suns (no reservations accepted - opens at 6 pm)
